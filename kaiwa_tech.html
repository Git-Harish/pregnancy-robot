Kaiwa Technology

Founded by Dr. Zhang Qifeng in Guangzhou, China

Affiliated with Nanyang Technological University

The company is developing a humanoid robot with an artificial womb embedded in its abdomen, designed to carry a fetus for ten months and deliver a baby.

2025
Concept presented at the World Robot Conference in Beijing
2026
Projected debut of the first prototype
~$14,000
Expected cost (100,000 yuan) - significantly less than human surrogacy

According to Dr. Zhang: "The artificial womb technology is already in a mature stage, and now it needs to be implanted in the robot's abdomen."
