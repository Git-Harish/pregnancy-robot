Potential Benefits and Applications

Alternative for Infertile Couples

Provides a new option for those unable to conceive or carry pregnancies naturally, at a significantly lower cost than traditional surrogacy ($14,000 vs. $100,000-$200,000).

Medical Applications

Could help women with medical conditions that make pregnancy dangerous or impossible, while maintaining genetic connection to their children.

Research Advancement

May provide new insights into fetal development and pregnancy complications through continuous monitoring and data collection.

Premature Birth Solutions

Technology could eventually help address premature birth complications by providing a controlled environment for development.

Sources: New York Post, Motherly, Interesting Engineering (2025)
